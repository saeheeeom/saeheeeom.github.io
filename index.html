GuardianDream: Adversarial Defense Against Diffusion-based Deepfake Generation

Saehee Eom, et al.

In Preparation | 2024

Developed a novel adversarial defense mechanism that protects personal photos from unauthorized deepfake generation. By injecting imperceptible perturbations into training images, the system degrades the quality and facial similarity of diffusion model outputs, effectively hindering identity misuse while maintaining original image quality for legitimate use.

July 2025

Achieved significant improvements in BERTScore (+23%) and perplexity (-22%) across multiple LLM architectures.
